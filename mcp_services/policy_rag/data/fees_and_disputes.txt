=== FEE AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION POLICIES ===

FD-001: Standard Fee Waiver Eligibility
Fee waivers are available for the following charges under defined conditions:
- Non-maintenance charges: Waived once per financial year for Classic
  segment customers with a satisfactory relationship history of 2 years.
- NEFT/RTGS charges: Waived for Premium and Wealth segment customers
  on all transactions.
- Demand Draft issuance charges: Waived for Wealth segment customers.
- SMS alert charges: Waived for all customers maintaining AMB above
  the minimum tier requirement for 6 consecutive months.
Fee waivers beyond the above require relationship manager approval and
must comply with GR-002 exception handling rules.

FD-002: Dispute Filing Process
Customers may raise a transaction dispute within 60 days of the
transaction date.
Disputes must be submitted through the mobile banking application,
net banking portal, or by visiting a branch in person.
Telephonic disputes are accepted for amounts above Rs. 10,000 only
and must be followed by written confirmation within 7 days.
Each dispute is assigned a unique reference number which the customer
must retain for follow-up purposes.

FD-003: Dispute Resolution Timelines
Credit card transaction disputes: Resolved within 45 days of filing.
UPI and IMPS disputes: Resolved within 7 business days.
NEFT and RTGS disputes: Resolved within 5 business days.
ATM cash disputes: Resolved within 7 business days.
If resolution is delayed beyond the stated timeline, the customer
is entitled to a compensation of Rs. 100 per day of delay, subject
to a maximum of Rs. 10,000.

FD-004: Goodwill Adjustments
Goodwill adjustments are available for customers who have experienced
a service failure attributable to the bank.
Maximum goodwill adjustment for Classic segment: Rs. 500 per instance.
Maximum goodwill adjustment for Premium segment: Rs. 2,000 per instance.
Maximum goodwill adjustment for Wealth segment: Rs. 5,000 per instance.
Goodwill adjustments are limited to 2 instances per customer per
financial year.
Goodwill adjustments require approval from the branch manager or
senior relationship manager and must be logged in the CRM system.
Goodwill adjustments cannot be used to waive mandatory regulatory
charges or penalties.

FD-005: Escalation Matrix
Level 1: Customer service agent — resolution within 3 business days.
Level 2: Branch manager or senior agent — resolution within 7 business days.
Level 3: Nodal officer — resolution within 15 business days.
Level 4: Banking Ombudsman — available after exhausting internal escalation.
Customers must receive written acknowledgement within 24 hours of
any escalation reaching Level 2 or above.
