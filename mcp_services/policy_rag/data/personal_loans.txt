=== PERSONAL LOAN POLICIES ===

PL-001: Personal Loan Eligibility
Minimum net monthly income for salaried applicants: Rs. 30,000.
Minimum tenure with current employer: 1 year.
Minimum credit score for personal loan approval: 680.
Applicants with a credit score between 650 and 679 may be considered
at a higher interest rate with additional income documentation, subject
to manual underwriting approval.
Applicants with a credit score below 650 are ineligible for personal loans.
Maximum age at loan maturity: 60 years for salaried applicants.
Maximum age at loan maturity: 65 years for self-employed applicants.
Applicants with high repayment risk are ineligible for personal loan
disbursement without a mandatory credit committee review.

PL-002: Loan Amount and Tenure
Maximum loan amount for salaried applicants: 20 times net monthly salary,
subject to an absolute cap of Rs. 40,00,000.
Maximum loan amount for self-employed applicants: 10 times average monthly
income, subject to an absolute cap of Rs. 25,00,000.
Minimum loan tenure: 12 months.
Maximum loan tenure: 60 months.
EMI to net monthly income ratio must not exceed 50% including all existing
loan obligations.
Customers with existing loans where the EMI-to-income ratio already exceeds
40% will be subject to enhanced scrutiny.

PL-003: Interest Rate Structure
Interest rates for personal loans are determined by credit score band,
customer segment, and repayment risk classification.
Customers with repayment risk classified as high will be offered rates
at the top of the applicable band or declined, at the discretion of
the underwriting team.
Rate concessions of up to 0.5% are available for Premium segment customers
with a credit score above 750 and low repayment risk.
Rate concessions for Wealth segment customers with low repayment risk
may extend to 1% below the standard rate, subject to credit committee approval.

PL-004: Prepayment and Foreclosure
Prepayment is permitted after a minimum of 6 EMI payments have been made.
Foreclosure charges: 4% of outstanding principal if closed within the
first 12 months of disbursement.
Foreclosure charges: 2% of outstanding principal if closed between
13 and 24 months from disbursement.
No foreclosure charges apply after 24 months of successful repayment.
Premium and Wealth segment customers with a low repayment risk classification
are exempt from foreclosure charges at any point during the loan tenure.
Customers with high repayment risk are not eligible for this exemption
regardless of customer segment.

PL-005: Top-Up Loan Eligibility
Existing personal loan customers may apply for a top-up loan after
12 months of regular repayment with zero missed EMIs.
Top-up eligibility is subject to re-assessment of current credit score
and repayment risk at the time of application.
Customers whose repayment risk has increased to high since original
disbursement are ineligible for top-up loans.
