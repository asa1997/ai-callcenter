=== CUSTOMER SEGMENT AND EXCEPTION POLICIES ===

CS-001: Segment Definitions
Classic Segment: Customers with AMB between Rs. 10,000 and Rs. 99,999
or total relationship value below Rs. 5,00,000.
Premium Segment: Customers with AMB between Rs. 1,00,000 and Rs. 9,99,999
or total relationship value between Rs. 5,00,000 and Rs. 49,99,999.
Wealth Segment: Customers with AMB of Rs. 10,00,000 and above
or total relationship value of Rs. 50,00,000 and above.
Total relationship value includes savings balances, fixed deposits,
mutual fund holdings, and insurance premiums held with the bank.
Customer segment is reviewed quarterly and may be upgraded or downgraded
based on the latest relationship value assessment.

CS-002: Premium Segment Benefits
Dedicated relationship manager assigned to each Premium customer.
Priority customer service queue across all channels.
Rate concessions on personal loans of up to 0.5% for customers with
credit score above 750 and low repayment risk.
Automatic annual fee waiver on primary credit card if spends exceed
Rs. 1,00,000 (reduced threshold from standard Rs. 1,50,000).
Free demand drafts up to 5 per month.
Premium customers with 12 months of clean payment history are eligible
for automatic credit limit enhancement of up to 50% subject to
credit score above 750 at time of enhancement.

CS-003: Wealth Segment Benefits
Private banking relationship manager and team assigned.
Automatic annual fee waiver on all credit cards irrespective of spend.
Rate concessions on home loans and personal loans up to 1% below
standard rate for customers with low repayment risk.
Free RTGS, NEFT, and demand drafts with no volume limit.
Dedicated wealth desk for investment and insurance advisory.
Wealth customers with low repayment risk are exempt from foreclosure
charges on personal loans at any point during the loan tenure.

CS-004: Long-Standing Customer Benefits
Customers with a banking relationship of 10 years or more are
classified as long-standing customers irrespective of segment.
Long-standing customers with a satisfactory account conduct history
are eligible for one fee waiver per financial year beyond the standard
segment entitlement, subject to relationship manager approval.
Long-standing customers with medium repayment risk may be considered
for personal loan approval on a case-by-case basis with enhanced
documentation, subject to credit committee review and GR-002 compliance.
Long-standing customer status does not override mandatory risk checks
or minimum credit score requirements under any circumstances.

CS-005: Exception Approval Matrix
All exceptions to standard eligibility criteria must follow this matrix:
- Exceptions for Classic segment customers: Branch manager approval required.
- Exceptions for Premium segment customers: Senior relationship manager approval.
- Exceptions for Wealth segment customers: Regional credit head approval.
No exception, regardless of customer segment or relationship value,
may override the minimum credit score thresholds defined in GR-001.
No exception may be granted to a customer with high fraud risk without
written approval from the fraud operations head.
All exceptions must be documented in the CRM system with a clear
rationale and the approving authority's employee ID.
