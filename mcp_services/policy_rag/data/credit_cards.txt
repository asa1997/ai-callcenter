=== CREDIT CARD POLICIES ===

CC-001: Credit Card Eligibility
Salaried applicants must have a minimum net monthly income of Rs. 25,000.
Self-employed applicants must demonstrate a minimum annual ITR of Rs. 4,00,000.
Minimum credit score for standard credit card issuance is 700.
Applicants with a credit score between 650 and 699 are eligible for a
secured credit card only, requiring a fixed deposit of minimum Rs. 25,000.
Applicants with a credit score below 650 are ineligible for any credit
card product including secured cards.
Minimum age at application is 21 years. Maximum age is 60 years.
Applicants must have a minimum of 1 year of continuous employment or
2 years of self-employment history.

CC-002: Credit Limit Assignment
Initial credit limit for Classic segment customers: Up to 3 times net monthly salary.
Initial credit limit for Premium segment customers: Up to 5 times net monthly salary.
Initial credit limit for Wealth segment customers: Up to 8 times net monthly salary,
subject to a maximum of Rs. 25,00,000.
Credit limits are subject to bureau assessment and internal risk scoring.

CC-003: Credit Limit Enhancement
Customers may request a credit limit enhancement after 12 months of
continuous card usage with the bank.
Eligibility requires zero missed payments in the last 12 months.
Minimum credit score of 720 is required at the time of enhancement request.
Premium and Wealth segment customers with a credit score above 750 and
a clean payment history of 12 months are eligible for automatic enhancement
of up to 50% of existing limit.
Classic segment customers require manual review for all enhancement requests
regardless of credit score.
Customers with high repayment risk are not eligible for credit limit enhancement
under any customer segment and require mandatory manual review.

CC-004: Annual Fee Waiver
Standard annual fee waiver is applicable if total annual card spends exceed
Rs. 1,50,000 in the preceding 12 months.
Wealth segment customers receive an automatic annual fee waiver irrespective
of spend levels.
First year fee waiver is available for applicants joining through the
corporate salary account program or employee banking channel.
Fee waiver requests outside standard criteria require approval from the
relationship manager and are subject to GR-002 exception handling rules.

CC-005: Card Suspension and Cancellation
Cards will be suspended immediately if fraud risk is reclassified to high.
Suspended cards require manual review before reinstatement.
Customers disputing a suspension may escalate to the nodal officer within
30 days of the suspension notice.
Card cancellation requires written confirmation from the primary cardholder.
