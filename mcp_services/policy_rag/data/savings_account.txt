=== SAVINGS & CURRENT ACCOUNT POLICIES ===

SA-001: Account Tiers
The bank offers three savings account tiers: Classic, Premium, and Wealth.
Classic accounts require a minimum average monthly balance (AMB) of Rs. 10,000.
Premium accounts require a minimum AMB of Rs. 1,00,000.
Wealth accounts require a minimum AMB of Rs. 10,00,000.
Failure to maintain minimum balance attracts a non-maintenance charge of Rs. 500
per month for Classic, Rs. 750 for Premium, and Rs. 1,000 for Wealth accounts.

SA-002: Account Upgrade Eligibility
Customers may apply for an account tier upgrade after a minimum of 6 months
of relationship with the bank.
Upgrade from Classic to Premium requires maintaining AMB above Rs. 1,00,000
for 3 consecutive months.
Upgrade from Premium to Wealth requires maintaining AMB above Rs. 10,00,000
for 3 consecutive months and a minimum credit score of 720.
Account upgrades are subject to a satisfactory risk profile assessment.
Customers flagged with medium or high fraud risk are ineligible for automatic
account upgrades and require manual review by the relationship team.

SA-003: Account Downgrade Rules
Accounts will be reviewed for downgrade if AMB falls below the tier minimum
for 3 consecutive months.
Customers will receive a written notice 30 days before a downgrade is actioned.
Downgrade from Wealth to Premium or Premium to Classic does not require
customer consent but must be communicated in writing.

SA-004: Dormancy Policy
An account is classified as dormant if there are no customer-initiated
transactions for 12 consecutive months.
Dormant accounts are frozen for debit transactions.
Reactivation requires the customer to visit a branch with valid KYC documents
or submit a reactivation request via the verified mobile banking application.
Dormant accounts with zero balance for 24 months may be closed by the bank
after due notice.

SA-005: Joint Account Rules
Joint accounts may be operated on Either or Survivor basis or Former or Survivor basis.
All joint account holders must independently satisfy KYC requirements.
Account tier upgrade for joint accounts requires the primary account holder
to meet eligibility criteria.
